Frequently Asked Questions

What is eKYC?
eKYC (Electronic Know Your Customer) is a digital process of identity verification that uses blockchain technology to securely verify and store customer information.
How secure is the eKYC process?
Our eKYC process is highly secure as it leverages blockchain technology, encryption, and multiple layers of verification to protect your data.
What documents do I need for eKYC?
You'll need a valid government ID (such as Passport, Driver's License, or National ID) and proof of address (utility bill or bank statement).
How long does the verification process take?
The verification process typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete, and you'll receive confirmation within 24 hours.
