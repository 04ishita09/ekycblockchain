KYC Instructions

1. Registration

Begin by creating your account with basic details. Make sure to use a valid email address and set a strong password.

2. Document Preparation

Keep the following documents ready in digital format:

  • Valid Government ID (Passport/Driver's License)
  • Recent Proof of Address
  • Latest Photograph

3. Document Upload

Upload clear, high-resolution scans or photos of your documents. Ensure all text is legible and photos are recent.

4. Verification

Our system will verify your documents using blockchain technology. This ensures maximum security and authenticity.

5. Track Status

Use your login credentials to track the status of your KYC verification at any time.

Important Notes

  • All information provided must be accurate and current
  • Documents should be valid and not expired
  • Processing typically takes 24-48 hours
